#+title: Race Condition
#+filetags: :computer-science:operating-system:concurrency:

A race condition is the condition that the behavior of the program is
dependent on the sequence or timing of some uncontrollable event.

The most common race condition in concurrent programming is [[id:0163335d-6745-4f9c-94e5-0450dad505ab][data race]].
