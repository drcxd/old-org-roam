#+title: Data Race
#+filetags: :computer-science:concurrency:

Data race is a kind of [[id:61f3fd7f-7f07-45b6-9716-4d0108e4998b][race condition]] when two or more flows of
control interfer with one another when accessing [[id:4339c01d-1bfc-4beb-8d6c-155514a73041][shared data]].
