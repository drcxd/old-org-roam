#+title: Atomic Operation
#+filetags: :computer-science:concurrency:

An atomic operation refers to a [[id:f9c52f78-fd4b-475a-9b56-01e72e483da2][critical operation]] that can not be
interrupted when operating on [[id:4339c01d-1bfc-4beb-8d6c-155514a73041][shared data]] by other critical operations
of the same shared data.
