#+title: Exchange
#+filetags: :computer-science:concurrency:

Exchange is an [[id:a36d93f8-6e97-45a7-950a-6ec170d558d7][Atomic Read-Modify-Write]] instruction which swap the
contents of two registers, or a register and a memory location.
